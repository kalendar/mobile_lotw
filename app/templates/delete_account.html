Requesting Account Deletion

If you wish to have your Mobile LotW account and associated data deleted, please send an email to info@mobilelotw.org including the following information:

  • Your callsign
  • A statement saying you would like your account and associated information deleted.
Your account and associated data will be deleted within five (5) business days of receipt.
